B.Sc. Agriculture (B.Sc. Ag)

Four-year (8 Semester) program focused on sustainable farming, research, and agribusiness.

Program Overview

The B.Sc. Agriculture program is dedicated to developing the next generation of agricultural leaders, scientists, and entrepreneurs. Our curriculum provides a holistic understanding of agricultural science, from soil health and crop production to animal husbandry and economics.

With extensive practical work on our research farms and field visits to key agricultural centers, students gain the hands-on skills necessary to build a sustainable and food-secure future for Nepal.
