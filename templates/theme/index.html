A Legacy of Excellence in Eastern Nepal

Established under the Madan Bhandari Memorial Academy Nepal, our School of Science and Technology is dedicated to realizing the vision of a prosperous Nepal through technical education. We combine rigorous academics with real-world application.

Located in Urlabari, Morang, our sprawling campus provides the perfect calm environment for focused studies while being accessible to the vibrant communities of Province 1.
